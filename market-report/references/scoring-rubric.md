## Revenue Impact Calculation Reference

Use this formula for each recommendation:

```
Conservative monthly impact =
  (Estimated % improvement) × (Current relevant metric) × (Deal value) × (Confidence factor)

Confidence factors:
- High confidence (proven tactic, similar client result): 0.7
- Medium confidence (strong indicator but unproven for this client): 0.5
- Low confidence (theoretical improvement): 0.3

Example — CRO improvement:
- Current traffic: 10,000 visits/month
- Current conversion: 2.0%
- Projected conversion after CRO: 3.0% (conservative from 1.8% industry benchmark gap)
- Average deal value: $500
- Monthly impact: (3% - 2%) × 10,000 × $500 = $50,000/month
- Confidence factor: 0.5 (medium)
- Conservative estimate: $25,000/month additional revenue
```

Always present as a range: "Estimated $15,000-$25,000/month additional revenue" rather than a single point estimate.
