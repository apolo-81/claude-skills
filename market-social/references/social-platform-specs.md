## Hashtag Strategy by Platform

### Tiered Hashtag Approach

| Tier | Post Count Range | Count to Use | Purpose |
|------|-----------------|-------------|---------|
| Niche | Under 100K posts | 3-5 | Highly targeted; easier to rank and be discovered |
| Mid-size | 100K–1M posts | 3-5 | Moderate competition; relevant engaged audience |
| Broad | 1M+ posts | 2-3 | Discovery potential; lower per-post engagement rate |
| Branded | Custom | 1 | Brand recognition; UGC collection; community building |

### Platform-Specific Hashtag Counts
- Instagram: 5-15 hashtags (caption or first comment; 8-10 is often optimal)
- LinkedIn: 3-5 hashtags (bottom of post; more looks spammy)
- Twitter/X: 1-2 hashtags (inline or at end; more hurts engagement)
- TikTok: 3-5 hashtags (in caption; trending hashtags can accelerate discovery)
- Facebook: 2-5 hashtags (optional; limited discovery benefit)

### Hashtag Research Process
For each content pillar, document:
- 5 niche hashtags for the brand's sub-industry
- 5 mid-size hashtags for the broader industry
- 3 broad hashtags for general discovery
- 1 branded hashtag (e.g., #BrandNameTips, #[ProductName]Community)

Refresh hashtag sets every 90 days — popularity shifts and saturated tags lose effectiveness.

## Content Repurposing: The 1-to-10 Framework

Take ONE long-form anchor piece and extract 10+ social posts. This is the core efficiency principle of sustainable content marketing.

### Why Repurposing Works
Creating from scratch for every platform is inefficient and leads to inconsistent messaging. Repurposing from a single high-quality source ensures message coherence across channels, reduces creative fatigue, and compounds the value of each research or production session.

### The Repurposing Map

```
SOURCE (Anchor Content):
  Blog post / Podcast episode / YouTube video / Newsletter / Webinar

DERIVATIVES:
  1. LinkedIn text post — Lead insight or contrarian take
  2. Twitter thread — 5-7 key takeaways, numbered
  3. Instagram carousel — Main framework visualized as slides
  4. Instagram Reel — 30-second summary of the #1 point
  5. TikTok — Quick-tip format of the most surprising finding
  6. LinkedIn carousel — PDF slideshow of the full framework
  7. Twitter single tweet — The single most quotable line
  8. Instagram Story — Behind-the-scenes of creating the piece
  9. YouTube Short — 60-second condensed video version
  10. Facebook post — Discussion question based on the topic
  11. Quote graphic — Pull quote on branded template
  12. Email snippet — Adapted into a nurture email section
```

### Repurposing Schedule

| Day | Action |
|-----|--------|
| Day 1 | Publish the original anchor content |
| Day 1-2 | Share key insight on LinkedIn + Tweet thread |
| Day 3 | Instagram carousel + Reel from the framework |
| Day 5 | TikTok + YouTube Short |
| Day 7 | Different angle or secondary takeaway post |
| Day 10 | Engagement question related to the topic |
| Day 14 | "In case you missed it" reshare with new framing |

## Content Pillar Framework

Define 4-5 pillars that anchor all content. Recommended mix:

| Pillar | Type | Purpose | % of Content |
|--------|------|---------|-------------|
| 1 | Educational | Establish authority; provide standalone value | 40% |
| 2 | Behind-the-Scenes | Build trust; humanize the brand or founder | 20% |
| 3 | Social Proof | Build credibility; drive consideration | 15% |
| 4 | Engagement | Build community; signal to algorithm | 15% |
| 5 | Promotional | Drive revenue; announce offers and launches | 10% |

**Why this ratio?** The 40-10 educational-to-promotional ratio reflects audience tolerance for self-promotion. Audiences follow accounts that teach them things. They tolerate promotional content when it's infrequent and follows genuine value delivery. Brands that post too much promotional content see steady follower and engagement decline.

## Platform-Specific Algorithm Notes

### LinkedIn (2024-2025)
- Native content strongly outperforms external links
- Early engagement (first 90 minutes) determines post distribution
- Comments weighted more than likes — end posts with a question
- Creator mode + consistent posting builds profile reach over 3-6 months
- Dwell time matters: use line breaks and open loops to keep readers reading

### Instagram
- Reels reach new audiences; feed carousels retain existing followers
- Stories with interactive elements (polls, questions) feed into algorithmic signals
- Saves and shares weighted heavily; ask readers to save educational content
- Hashtags less important than they were — content quality drives discovery
- Posting to Stories daily maintains position in follower feeds

### TikTok
- Algorithm is primarily interest-based, not follower-based — new accounts can go viral
- Completion rate is the primary metric — hooks must capture in the first 2 seconds
- Trending audio dramatically increases reach in the first 72 hours of a trend
- Posting 1-3x daily during growth phase, then maintaining 1x daily
- Replies to comments as video content is a high-engagement format

### Twitter/X (2024-2025)
- Threads significantly outperform single tweets for reach
- Verified accounts (Premium) get algorithmic preference
- Engagement pods (coordinated replies) can game early signals
- Media (images, videos) increase impression rate by 2-3x
- Avoid external links in the main tweet; use in replies
