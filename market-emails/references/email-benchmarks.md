## Subject Line Formulas Reference

| Formula | Template | Example | Best For |
|---------|----------|---------|----------|
| Number + Benefit | "[N] ways to [result]" | "3 ways to double your conversion rate" | Educational content |
| Curiosity Gap | "The [thing] that [consequence]" | "The pricing mistake that cost me $50K" | Story-driven emails |
| Direct Benefit | "Your [thing] is ready" | "Your copy report is ready" | Delivery/welcome |
| Personalization + Urgency | "[Name], your [thing] [expiration]" | "[Name], your trial expires tomorrow" | Onboarding, urgency |
| Question | "Are you making this [topic] mistake?" | "Are you making this SEO mistake?" | Problem-awareness |
| How-To | "How to [result] in [timeframe]" | "How to write landing pages that convert at 10%" | Educational |
| Social Proof | "Why [N] [audience] switched this month" | "Why 5,000 marketers switched this month" | Nurture/launch |
| Urgency | "[Time] left: [offer]" | "Last chance: 40% off ends at midnight" | Launch/cart abandonment |
| Pattern Interrupt | "I was wrong about [topic]" | "I was wrong about email marketing" | Re-engagement |
| Negative Frame | "Stop [bad thing] that doesn't work" | "Stop wasting money on ads" | Problem-awareness |
| Confession | "I [embarrassing thing] and here's what I learned" | "I ignored my list for 6 months. Here's what happened." | Story, re-engagement |
| Insider | "The [topic] nobody talks about" | "The welcome email nobody sends" | Authority building |

**Subject line character targets:**
- Mobile cutoff: 40-50 characters
- Desktop cutoff: 60-70 characters
- Optimal for both: 35-45 characters

## A/B Testing Hierarchy

Test in this order — each variable has diminishing returns once optimized:

1. **Subject line** — biggest lever for open rate (test 2 variants per email)
2. **Offer and CTA** — biggest lever for click rate
3. **Send timing** — 10-20% swing depending on audience
4. **Email length** — test short (under 200 words) vs long (500+ words)
5. **Format** — plain text vs HTML vs hybrid
6. **Personalization** — with/without first name, dynamic content
7. **Images** — with/without, above/below fold

**Minimum sample size for statistical significance:** 1,000 recipients per variant at 95% confidence.

## Compliance Quick Reference

### CAN-SPAM (United States)
- Physical mailing address required in every commercial email
- Clear and conspicuous unsubscribe link (must be honored within 10 business days)
- "From" name and email address must accurately identify the sender
- Subject line must not be deceptive or misleading
- Must label the message as an advertisement if that is what it is

### GDPR (European Union / EEA)
- Requires explicit, documented opt-in consent (no pre-checked boxes)
- Must document when, how, and what the subscriber consented to
- Right to be forgotten: delete subscriber data on request within 30 days
- Data Processing Agreement required with every ESP
- Subscribers can request a copy of their data at any time
- Consent for marketing cannot be bundled with Terms of Service acceptance

### CASL (Canada)
- Express consent required for all commercial electronic messages
- Implied consent allowed for existing business relationships (24-month window)
- Unsubscribe mechanism must work within 10 business days
- Sender identification (name, mailing address, contact info) required
- No purchased or harvested email lists

### PECR (United Kingdom, post-Brexit)
- Similar to GDPR requirements; explicit consent required
- Soft opt-in rule: existing customers can be emailed about similar products
- Unsubscribe must be honored immediately

**Legal note:** Always advise users to verify compliance with their own legal counsel. Regulations change and vary by jurisdiction.

## Deliverability Checklist

Before launching any sequence, verify:
- [ ] Domain authenticated: SPF, DKIM, DMARC records configured
- [ ] Sending from a subdomain for marketing (e.g., hello@mail.company.com)
- [ ] List cleaned: remove hard bounces immediately, soft bounces after 3 attempts
- [ ] Warm up new IPs/domains gradually (start at 50/day, double weekly)
- [ ] Unsubscribe link works and processes within required timeframe
- [ ] Plain-text version of every HTML email is included
- [ ] Spam score tested (use Mail-Tester.com or GlockApps) before launch
- [ ] Seed list tested across major email clients (Gmail, Outlook, Apple Mail)
