<!-- SLIDE 5: Comparison (A vs B) -->

# Three changes transformed the first-hour experience

| Before (Manual) | After (Automated) |
|---|---|
| Empty dashboard on first login | Guided wizard with 3 key actions |
| CSR call scheduled 3-5 days out | In-app prompts within 5 minutes |
| Generic welcome email | Personalized sequence based on use case |

<!-- Notes:
[SLIDE 5 — 45 seconds]

EVIDENCE: Each of these individually improved activation. Together, the compound effect was the 34% churn reduction you saw.
