# Social Media Launch Templates

## Twitter/X — Launch Thread
```
Post 1/7:
After [X months] of building, [PRODUCT NAME] is live today.

[One-line value prop]: [Product] helps [audience] [achieve outcome] without [pain].

Here's the story of why I built it and what it can do for you:

🧵

Post 2/7:
The problem:

[Describe the problem vividly. Make it relatable. Specific pain point, specific frustration.]

[If you have a stat, use it: "X% of [audience] struggle with this..."]

Post 3/7:
The solution:

[What your product does in plain language.]

[Screenshot or demo GIF here]

The key insight: [What makes your approach different from how people currently solve this]

Post 4/7:
Early results from beta testers:

• [Beta user 1]: [Specific result in specific timeframe]
• [Beta user 2]: [Specific result]
• [Beta user 3]: [Specific result]

[Quote from one beta user if possible]

Post 5/7:
What's included:

• [Feature/benefit 1]
• [Feature/benefit 2]
• [Feature/benefit 3]
• [Feature/benefit 4]
• [Bonus or differentiator]

Post 6/7:
Launch offer (first [X] customers only):

[Price] → [Discounted price] — [Specific savings]
[Bonus 1] included (worth $[X])
[Bonus 2] included

Offer expires [specific date/time].

Post 7/7:
Get started now: [Link]

If you know someone who [pain point], tag them below.

[CTA question to drive engagement]
```

## LinkedIn — Launch Announcement Post
```
I just launched [PRODUCT NAME], and here's what made it necessary:

[1-2 sentences about the problem — make it relatable to your LinkedIn audience's professional context]

After [talking to X customers / X months building / experiencing this pain directly], I realized [core insight].

So I built [PRODUCT NAME] to [specific outcome].

In beta, [X] users have already seen:
— [Result 1 with specifics]
— [Result 2 with specifics]
— [Result 3 with specifics]

If you [describe target audience role/situation], I'd love for you to try it:
[Link]

We're offering [launch pricing] for the first [X] customers — valid through [date].

What questions do you have about [problem/topic area]? Happy to discuss in the comments.

#[industry] #[relevant] #[hashtag]
```

## Instagram — Launch Caption
```
[HOOK — first line must stop the scroll. Use a bold claim, surprising stat, or provocative question.]

The problem: [1-2 sentences, visceral and specific]

The solution: [PRODUCT NAME] — [one-line description]

Early users are seeing:
✓ [Result 1]
✓ [Result 2]
✓ [Result 3]

Launch offer: [Pricing] through [date only]

Link in bio to get started.

—

[10-15 targeted hashtags: mix of high-volume, medium, and niche]
```

**Image/Carousel guidance:**
- Slide 1: Bold hook text on branded background
- Slide 2: Problem visualization (before state)
- Slide 3: Solution screenshot or product demo
- Slide 4: Results/social proof (specific numbers)
- Slide 5: Offer + CTA

## Press Release Structure
1. Headline: [Company] Launches [Product] to Help [Audience] [Outcome]
2. Subheadline: [Supporting detail with a key stat or differentiator]
3. First paragraph: Who, what, when, where, why (the news in 1 paragraph)
4. Quote from founder/CEO
5. Product details and key features
6. Market context (why now, market size, relevant trend)
7. Customer quote or early results
8. Availability and pricing
9. About the company (boilerplate)
10. Contact information

[Name]
