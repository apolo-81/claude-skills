# Launch Metrics Framework

## Real-Time Launch Dashboard

Track these during launch week — check every 2-4 hours on Day 1, daily thereafter.

### Awareness Metrics
| Metric | Where to Check | Launch Day Target | Week 1 Target |
|---|---|---|---|
| Website sessions | Google Analytics / Plausible | 5-10x normal daily traffic | Based on list/channel size |
| Traffic by source | Analytics → Acquisition | Email #1 in top sources | Mix of email, social, direct |
| Social impressions | Native analytics per platform | 3-10x normal post reach | -- |
| Press mentions | Google Alerts | Any = win | -- |
| Email open rate | ESP dashboard | 30-50% (warm list) | -- |

### Engagement Metrics
| Metric | Benchmark | Good | Great |
|---|---|---|---|
| Time on site | Industry avg: 2-3 min | >3 min | >5 min |
| Pages per session | 1.5-2.0 | 2-3 | 3+ |
| Social engagement rate | 1-3% | 3-8% | 8%+ |
| Email CTR | 2-5% | 5-15% | 15%+ |
| Demo video completion | 30-40% | 50% | 70%+ |

### Conversion Metrics
| Metric | Benchmark | Track Daily |
|---|---|---|
| Landing page conversion rate | Cold traffic: 2-5% / Warm list: 10-30% | Yes |
| Email → purchase conversion | 0.5-2% per email | Yes |
| Revenue vs. target | 100% of goal | Yes |
| Average order value | vs. expected | Yes |
| Cost per acquisition (paid) | vs. LTV/3 target | Yes |

### Post-Launch Retention Metrics (Week 2+)
- Day 1 retention (product logins on Day 1)
- Day 7 retention
- Feature adoption rate (key action taken within 7 days)
- Support ticket volume (spike = onboarding issue)
- NPS score (survey at Day 7-14)

## Budget Allocation Guide

| Budget Level | Allocation |
|---|---|
| **Bootstrapped ($0-500)** | 100% organic: content, communities, email, personal outreach |
| **Moderate ($500-5,000)** | 40% paid ads, 30% influencer/partner, 20% tools, 10% design |
| **Well-Funded ($5,000-25,000)** | 35% paid ads, 25% influencer/partner, 20% PR/media, 10% events, 10% tools |
| **Enterprise ($25,000+)** | 30% paid ads, 20% events/webinars, 20% PR, 15% influencer, 10% content, 5% tools |

## Pre-Launch Checklist

### Technical (48 hours before launch)
- [ ] Landing page loads under 3 seconds on mobile
- [ ] Checkout / signup flow tested end-to-end on mobile AND desktop
- [ ] All UTM parameters configured and tracking correctly
- [ ] Analytics conversion goals firing correctly
- [ ] Email confirmation flow tested (opt-in → confirm → welcome)
- [ ] All social profiles updated with launch info in bio/links
- [ ] Live chat or support coverage confirmed for launch day
- [ ] FAQ document sent to support team
- [ ] Backup plan ready if primary payment/hosting fails

### Content (24 hours before launch)
- [ ] All emails scheduled in ESP (do not rely on manual send)
- [ ] All social posts drafted and scheduled (or ready to copy-paste)
- [ ] Partner swipe copy delivered and confirmed received
- [ ] Affiliate links tested and tracking correctly
- [ ] Press release distributed (if applicable)
- [ ] Real-time metrics dashboard bookmarked and accessible

### Day-of Launch Checklist (6 AM)
- [ ] Verify email #1 scheduled to send at correct time
- [ ] Confirm landing page is live and loading correctly
- [ ] Confirm checkout/signup is accepting transactions
- [ ] Notify team: "Launch is go"
- [ ] Monitor inbox for any immediate customer issues
- [ ] Post launch announcement on each social channel (if not scheduled)
